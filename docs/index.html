Welcome to the LooPindex!

The LooPindex is a digital catalog containing the pinning data of all irreducible, indecomposable multiloops in the unoriented sphere with at most 12 complementary regions. There are a total of 1097 such.

The notations and terminology often follow those introduced in the preprint
Pin the loop taut : a one-player topological game
by Christopher-Lloyd Simon and Ben Stucky, which is available on the arxiv.

The data on these pages is available in flexible formats upon request. The code used to generate everything seen here is available on GitHub.

Examples

The multiloops below are unique in that they are the only ones in the catalog with the property that every region has degree at least 3. This property appears to make their minimal pinning sets especially rich.
